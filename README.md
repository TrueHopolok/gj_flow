<h1>Drum Wave</h1>
<p>You are the best sumo wrestler in you home town of <strong>Mizukata</strong>.</p>
<p>After defeating everyone around, you set out on a new adventure, searching for new opponents. To pass the time while traveling, you pick up a new hobby:&nbsp;drumming.</p>
<p>Go with the <strong>flow</strong>, ride the <strong>wave</strong>, defend from treacherous creatures of the sea (and sky!) and find new challenges <strong>beyond</strong>.</p>
<hr>
